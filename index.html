The real problem

Going vegan is easy. Eating out as a vegan is the hard part.

If you're vegan, you already know the drill. Menus don't label anything, "veg" doesn't mean vegan, and Google Maps lies. VeganFinds was built by someone who's navigated this for 20 years.

01

Hidden, undiscoverable dishes

Plenty of dishes are accidentally vegan — but nobody tells you. Even "veg" rice can be stored in dairy butter. You're guessing every single time.

02

The awkward group dinner

Out with non-vegan friends, everyone's waiting, and you feel like a burden. So you rush, settle, or skip eating out entirely.

03

Misleading & "expensive" labels

Maps point to veg, not vegan. Owners mislabel for footfall. And the few real vegan spots feel premium — making veganism look unaffordable.

How VeganFinds knows

Every dish earns a TrustScore.

Not a vibe — a number. Each dish is scored across three layers that get sharper as the community grows. The bigger we get, the more accurate your next meal.

1

Rule engine classification

Our moat: 20 years of lived vegan knowledge, encoded. It reads public menus from Zomato & Maps and flags vegan, adaptable, or not — exceptions included.

2

Expert verification

Our internal team and trusted vegan experts cross-check dishes and add exactly how to adapt each one. Verified dishes score higher.

3

Community feedback

You tell us if it was truly vegan, if counters were separate, if the cutlery smelled of dairy. Averaged across users, it keeps every score honest and live.

Restaurant not in our database?

Point your camera at the menu. Our AI — trained on the rule engine to cut hallucinations — tells you what's vegan and how to ask. Every scan feeds the database back.

What you get

Built for how you actually eat out.

Open the app, pick where you are, and order. No toggling between five tabs of Happy Cow, Maps and reviews.

Nearby, instantly

GPS finds the eateries around you and renders a clean menu showing only vegan and adaptable dishes. Non-vegan items are hidden.

Exactly what to say

Tap any adaptable dish for the precise customisation — in the local language — so the kitchen actually gets it right.

AI menu scanner

No data on a place? Scan the physical menu and our AI, tuned by our rule engine, reads it dish by dish — with a clear AI disclaimer.

Honest TrustScore

A transparent accuracy score on every dish — no inflated ratings, no mislabelled "vegan" spots to waste your evening.

Powered by the community

Every meal you log makes the app smarter for the next vegan. Real crowdsourced data, not paid placements.

Affordable by default
